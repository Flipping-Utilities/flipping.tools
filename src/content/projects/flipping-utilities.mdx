## Overview

Flipping Utilities is a comprehensive multi-platform flipping toolkit.
It provides a suite of tools for OSRS Grand Exchange trading across RuneLite, Discord and its website with seamless synchronization.

## Features

- **Multi-platform support** - Available as RuneLite plugin, Discord bot, discord server and website.
- **Cross-platform sync** - Data synced across all platforms so you can view your offers and be notified when they're filled or undercut.
- **Recipe support** - Analyze margins on combined items and processed goods
- **Portfolio tracking** - Track your investments and overall profit
- **Price history** - View historical price charts for any item
- **Price alerts** - Get notified when items hit your target prices
- **Trend detection** - Identify market trends and opportunities
- **Custom indexes** - Create your own market indexes to customize your experience.

## Platforms

### RuneLite Plugin

The RuneLite plugin integrates directly into the game client, providing:
- In-game price overlays on Grand Exchange offers
- Profit tracking for all purchased and sold items
- Price and price history viewing for all items
- Notification system for completed trades

### Website

The price site at [prices.osrs.cloud](https://prices.osrs.cloud/) offers:
- Full item database with live prices
- Interactive price charts
- Portfolio management
- Recipe calculator
- Trend analysis tools
- And more

### Discord Bot

Add the bot to your server for:
- Price check commands
- Price trend viewing 
- Flip finder and recommendations
- Price alerts via DM

### Discord Server

Join the [Flipping Discord](https://discord.gg/flipping) community for:
- Discussion and tips
- Price alerts
- Market updates
- Support
- Community strategies

## Pricing

Flipping Utilities operates on a freemium model. Core features are available for free, with premium features available to subscribers.
